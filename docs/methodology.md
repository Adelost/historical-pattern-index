# Methodology & Scoring

HPI calculates scores based on **Binary Checklists** (Yes=1, No=0). Scores are expressed as percentages (0-100%).

## 1. Systematic Intensity Score (Y-Axis)
*Measure of intent and organization.*

1.  **Policy:** Explicit orders, laws, or decrees authorizing violence?
2.  **State Involvement:** Direct involvement of military/state institutions?
3.  **Infrastructure:** Purpose-built camps, prisons, or transport?
4.  **Propaganda:** Organized dehumanization campaigns?
5.  **Broad Targeting:** Violence against civilians (women/children)?
6.  **Cultural Ban:** Legal prohibition of language/religion?
7.  **Property Seizure:** Systematic confiscation of assets?
8.  **Identification:** Registry/ID systems used to target victims?
9.  **Biological Warfare:** Starvation/disease/sterilization as weapons?
10. **Duration:** System persisted >5 years?

## 2. Profit Score (Driver)
*Measure of economic incentive.*

1.  **Direct Revenue:** Did the event generate measurable revenue for the state/entity?
2.  **Resource Extraction:** Was the primary goal raw materials (gold, rubber, land)?
3.  **Forced Labor:** Was slave or coerced labor used?
4.  **Economic Dependence:** Was the economy dependent on this system?
5.  **Market Integration:** Were goods sold to consumer markets?

## 3. Ideology Score (Driver)
*Measure of conviction.*

1.  **Purity Ideal:** Goal of ethnic/religious cleansing?
2.  **Historical Claim:** Justified by "ancient rights" or destiny?
3.  **Higher Purpose:** Framed as necessary for national survival/salvation?
4.  **Victim Narrative:** Perpetrators claimed self-defense?
5.  **Utopianism:** Promise of a better future post-cleansing?

## 4. Complicity Score (Enabler)
*Why did society allow it?*

1.  **Distance:** Geographic separation from the atrocity?
2.  **Benefit:** Did the public benefit economically?
3.  **Euphemisms:** Use of sanitizing language?
4.  **Diffused Responsibility:** Unclear chain of command?
5.  **Silence:** Punishment for dissenters?
